kuwait

"Kuwait is distinguished by many tourist attractions that impress both children and adults, thanks to its long history that exceeds 380 years and rapid development that has not abandoned its ancient heritage.". Among these attractions: "Dar Al-Athar Al-Islamiyyah": The Islamic Antiquities House (DAI) or Museum of Islamic Art is a government institution based on the collection of Islamic art from private sources. It was founded by Sheikh Nasser Al-Sabah Al-Ahmad Al-Sabah since 1975, and its ownership was transferred to the Kuwaiti government in 1983 to exhibit its holdings in the National Museum of Kuwait. The Islamic Artifacts House contains over 30,000 artistic pieces dating back to the period between the 8th and 18th centuries AD, covering all Islamic regions from Andalusia in Spain to the borders of China. It represents the essence of the Islamic Artifacts House, where a variety of cultural activities take place.

Singapore

Singapore is a small Asian country consisting of about 60 small islands, in addition to the main island which occupies an area of 28.9 km2, located to the north of the Malaysian peninsula, separated by the Johor Strait. Singapore's exceptional location in the southernmost part of the Malay Peninsula has contributed to its prosperity and development. It controls the Malacca Strait, which connects the South China Sea and the Indian Ocean. The country grew from a British colony to become a member of the Commonwealth, and it is home to the largest port in Southeast Asia and one of the world's busiest airports.

malaysia

It is a country located in Southeast Asia, consisting of 13 states and three federal territories, with a total area of ​​329,845 square kilometers. The capital is Kuala Lumpur, while Putrajaya is the headquarters of the federal government. The population reaches more than 30 million people in 2014. The country is divided into two parts separated by the South China Sea, namely the Malaysian Peninsula and Malaysian Borneo (also known as East Malaysia). The Malaysian monarch is Yang di-Pertuan Agong, while the Prime Minister heads the government. The government closely adopts the Westminster parliamentary system.
